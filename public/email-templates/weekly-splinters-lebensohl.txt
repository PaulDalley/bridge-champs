Forcing Declarer & The Power of Pass

Forcing declarer is one of the most valuable defence skills you can learn. It teaches what trumps are really for in a suit contract: control and communication, not just extra tricks.

On the bidding side this week, we are working on The Power of Pass. This is where many players get stuck: not knowing whether partner's bid is forcing, or passing unexpectedly when they should continue describing their hand.

---
Bidding Stage 2 — Problem 20
Theme: The Power of Pass

Auction:
| S  | W    | N  | E  | S |
| 1D | Pass | 1S | 2H | ? |

You (South)
S  7 5
H  A Q 10
D  A Q 9 4 3
C  J 8 2

What should South bid now?
This is a very instructive spot. We expect 90%+ of players to get this one wrong the first time through.
