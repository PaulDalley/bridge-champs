Forcing Declarer & The Power of Pass

Forcing declarer
"My tip for any player wanting to take their bridge to the next level, I see players of all levels get this wrong consistently" Paul

Forcing declarer is one of the most valuable defence skills you can learn. It teaches what trumps are really for in a suit contract: control and communication, not just extra tricks.

Power of Pass
On the bidding side this week, we are working on The Power of Pass. Ask yourself - have I shown my hand? Would partner be able to guess the approximate nature of my hand - If yes, pass is often going to be the correct bid.

Bidding Stage 2 — Problem 20
Theme: The Power of Pass

Auction:

                  1D

S  7 5 4

What should South bid now?
