Splinters & Lebensohl

Splinters are a bidding tool worth getting right. It's also important to evaluate your hand opposite partner's shortage. This skill carries into many other auctions.

Bridge is won and lost in the partscores. Lebensohl is a useful tool for competing effectively. We're also looking at when we should and shouldn't compete, and what other bidding options we have.

Bidding Stage 3 — Problem 2

Auction: 1S — Pass — 4D — Pass — ?

You (South)
S  A K 10 9 8 7
H  K 5
D  9 8 7 6
C  A

Given this bidding, how are you feeling about your hand?
