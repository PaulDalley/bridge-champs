WHY NOT “JUST FORWARD” A FANCY HTML EMAIL?
------------------------------------------
Forwarding usually wrecks layout: nested quote blocks, stripped <style> tags,
fonts that never load, images blocked, and different colours in Outlook vs
Gmail vs Apple Mail. Ron’s list may also be plain-text or a simple mailer.
