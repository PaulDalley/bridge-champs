Adelaide National Championship Pairs

Paul Dalley from Bridgechampions.com has put together 5 practice bidding questions as warmup, teaching how to score well in matchpoint pairs.

With thousands of dollars in prize money on the line, every board matters.

Paul's Principle

When our side has the points (22+)

Bid conservatively.

When they have the points, or they're split evenly (20 or less)

Bid aggressively.

Most players find that surprising!
