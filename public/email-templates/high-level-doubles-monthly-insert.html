Consider this auction

Auction:

N E S W
  1♠ X 4♠
X P ?  

South hand: ♠2   ♥KQ104   ♦A1032   ♣A942

Paul's 1st rule about doubles: When the opponent's bid and raise a suit, double should not mean "penalty" in the classical sense, where we have a lot of their suit. It is much more useful and frequent to play it as takeout oriented.

Think about it, in the given auction the opponent's often have 9-10 card fit, how many spades can we really have? The double is a very important bid, assigning it as a strict "penalty" is too inflexible.

However, does that mean we have to bid since partner has made a takeout double?

Paul's 2nd rule: It's easier to defend a 4 level contract than to make a 5 level contract.

It's often at least fine to defend on the 4 level when your side has the majority of the points, especially when the alternative is to move to the 5 level. On this hand I do not expect partner to have a penalty hand, I'm expecting say 2 spades in partner's hand. Yet, I'll leave the double in and try to make 4+ tricks rather than 11 tricks I need on the 5 level.

I would bid rather declare than defend if I had a void in spades.

Let's change the auction slightly (Problem 31)

Auction:

N E S W
  3♥ X 4♥
X P ?  

South hand (first hand): ♠J7642   ♥2   ♦AK104   ♣AK4

Here there is the option of defending on the 4 level or playing on the 4 level (not the 5 level). This becomes a more serious consideration, with an appropriate hand, it can certainly be right to just declare (a singleton in their suit is a good reason to declare).

Let's change the hand slgihtly, same auction

Auction:

N E S W
  3♥ X 4♥
X P ?  

South hand: ♠KQ10   ♥K10   ♦A1043   ♣Q1092

Compare that with having a bit more length/strength in their suit, and all of a sudden its much more appealing to defend

An overall summary- When deciding whether to bid or defend on the 4 level, look at your holding in the opponent's suit, if you have points in their suit, its often correct to defend, if you have a singleton or void in their suit, its often correct to declare. But you generally need a void to make it worth your while going to the 5 level.
