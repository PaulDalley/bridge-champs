Paul's rule

Points are misfitting when you have values, say 5+ points, opposite partner's singleton or void. This is a warning sign ⚠️ - KEEP THE AUCTION LOW - Your hands are misfitting. Slam is MUCH less likely and 3NT is probably your best spot (or 4 of a major).
